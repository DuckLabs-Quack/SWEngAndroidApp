Design

Preliminary design on paper

We started with the homepage as the central part of the app. The homepage needed to showcase the main attribute of our app which is the recipes and the recipe viewer so we put them forward on this. However we also thought it might be necessary for the user to return easily to previously seen recipe, so we made a highlight reel for them in the homepage. We wanted the search page to be very accessible from the home screen, if the user has a specific dish it mind the first thing he will want to do is to access the search page. Hence it needed to be quickly accessible form the home page so we place a search icon in the top left of the home page, with the other pages accessible from the top right. From there, we created the other pages we thought were necessary for the app. The shopping list for example we felt was a very important part of cooking app. Then moved on to the creating a recipe page which we thought would improve our product allowing users to share recipes together and our recipe database to grow.

Final version of the design on proto.io. We wanted a modern and clean look, so we changed the colour scheme and opted for a sharp color contrast for the logo and icons. To the homepage we added the idea of the recipe of the day to provide more suggestions to the users. We also realised that our target audience might need some help with some cooking techniques, hence we added the instruction videos page.
